Account & Login Help
====================

Password Reset
--------------
To reset your password:
1. Go to the login page at app.example.com/login
2. Click "Forgot Password?" below the sign-in button
3. Enter the email address associated with your account
4. Check your inbox for a reset link (valid for 24 hours)
5. Click the link and enter your new password (minimum 8 characters)
6. You will be automatically logged in after resetting

If you do not receive the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder.
If the issue persists, contact support@example.com.

Changing Your Email Address
---------------------------
To change the email on your account:
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to Settings → Account → Email
3. Enter your new email address and confirm your current password
4. A verification link will be sent to your new email
5. Click the link to confirm the change

Note: You cannot change your email to one already used by another account.

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
--------------------------------
We support authenticator apps (Google Authenticator, Authy) and SMS codes.

To enable 2FA:
1. Go to Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication
2. Click "Enable 2FA"
3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app or enter the setup key manually
4. Enter the 6-digit code from your app to confirm
5. Save your backup codes in a safe place

To disable 2FA, go to Settings → Security and click "Disable 2FA". You will need to enter a valid code from your authenticator app to confirm.

Locked Accounts
---------------
Your account will be temporarily locked after 5 failed login attempts.
The lockout lasts 30 minutes. After 30 minutes, you can try logging in again.
If you believe your account was locked by mistake, contact support.

Session Management
------------------
You can view all active sessions in Settings → Security → Active Sessions.
To log out of all other devices, click "Log out all other sessions".
Sessions expire automatically after 30 days of inactivity.
