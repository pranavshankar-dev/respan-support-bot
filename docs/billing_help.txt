Billing & Subscriptions
=======================

Plans and Pricing
-----------------
We offer three plans:

Starter (Free)
- Up to 3 projects
- 1,000 API calls/month
- Community support
- 7-day log retention

Pro ($29/month)
- Unlimited projects
- 50,000 API calls/month
- Email support (response within 24 hours)
- 90-day log retention
- Custom integrations

Enterprise (Custom pricing)
- Unlimited everything
- Dedicated support with SLA
- 1-year log retention
- SSO / SAML support
- Custom contracts and invoicing

Upgrading Your Plan
-------------------
To upgrade:
1. Go to Settings → Billing → Plans
2. Select the plan you want
3. Enter your payment details (credit card or ACH bank transfer)
4. Confirm the upgrade

Your new plan takes effect immediately. You will be charged a prorated amount
for the remainder of the current billing period.

Downgrading Your Plan
---------------------
To downgrade, go to Settings → Billing → Plans and select a lower tier.
Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.
If your current usage exceeds the limits of the lower plan, you will be
notified and must reduce usage before the downgrade takes effect.

Cancellation Policy
-------------------
You can cancel your subscription at any time from Settings → Billing → Cancel Plan.
Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period.
You will not receive a refund for unused time in the current period.
After cancellation, your account will revert to the Starter (Free) plan.
Your data will be retained for 30 days after cancellation.

Invoices and Receipts
---------------------
Invoices are emailed automatically on each billing date.
You can also download past invoices from Settings → Billing → Invoice History.
Invoices are in PDF format and include a breakdown of charges.

Failed Payments
---------------
If a payment fails, we will retry it automatically after 3, 5, and 7 days.
You will receive an email notification each time a payment attempt fails.
If the payment fails after all retries, your account will be downgraded to
the Free plan. To restore your Pro plan, update your payment method in
Settings → Billing → Payment Methods and pay the outstanding balance.

Refund Policy
-------------
Refunds are available within 7 days of the initial purchase or upgrade.
To request a refund, email billing@example.com with your account email
and the reason for the refund. Refunds are not available after 7 days.
