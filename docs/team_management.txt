Team Management & Permissions
==============================

Inviting Team Members
---------------------
To invite someone to your organization:
1. Go to Settings → Team → Members
2. Click "Invite Member"
3. Enter their email address
4. Select their role (see roles below)
5. Click "Send Invite"

The invitee will receive an email with a link valid for 48 hours.
If they do not accept within 48 hours, you can resend the invite from
Settings → Team → Pending Invites.

Roles and Permissions
---------------------
There are four roles:

Owner
- Full access to everything including billing and team management
- Can delete the organization
- Only one Owner per organization (can be transferred)

Admin
- Full access except billing and deleting the organization
- Can invite/remove members and change roles
- Can manage all projects and API keys

Member
- Can view and edit projects they are assigned to
- Cannot invite members or access billing
- Cannot create or delete API keys

Viewer
- Read-only access to assigned projects
- Cannot make any changes
- Useful for stakeholders who need visibility

Changing a Member's Role
------------------------
1. Go to Settings → Team → Members
2. Find the member and click the role dropdown next to their name
3. Select the new role
4. Confirm the change

Note: You cannot change the Owner's role without transferring ownership.

Removing a Team Member
-----------------------
1. Go to Settings → Team → Members
2. Find the member and click the three-dot menu (⋮)
3. Select "Remove from organization"
4. Confirm

Removed members lose access immediately. Their contributions (projects, logs)
remain in the organization. Any API keys they created remain active —
rotate them in Settings → API → API Keys if needed for security.

Transferring Ownership
-----------------------
To transfer the Owner role to another member:
1. Go to Settings → Team → Members
2. Find the member you want to make Owner
3. Click "Transfer Ownership"
4. Enter your password to confirm

You will become an Admin after the transfer.

Project-Level Access
---------------------
By default, Members can only see projects they are explicitly assigned to.
To assign a member to a project:
1. Open the project
2. Go to Project Settings → Access
3. Click "Add Member" and select from your team

Admins and Owners can see all projects regardless of assignment.

Single Sign-On (SSO)
---------------------
SSO via SAML 2.0 is available on the Enterprise plan only.

To configure SSO:
1. Go to Settings → Security → SSO
2. Click "Configure SSO"
3. Enter your Identity Provider (IdP) metadata URL or upload the XML file
4. Map your IdP attributes to Respan fields (email, name, role)
5. Test the connection before enabling

Once SSO is enabled, all members will be required to authenticate via your IdP.
You can still allow password login as a fallback in the SSO settings.

Audit Logs
----------
All team management actions are recorded in the audit log.
To view the audit log: Settings → Security → Audit Log

Audit log entries include: action type, actor, target, timestamp, and IP address.
Audit logs are retained for 90 days on Pro and 1 year on Enterprise.
